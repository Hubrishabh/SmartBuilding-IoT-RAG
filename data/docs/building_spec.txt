Building Specification (Excerpt)

Zones & Sensors:
- Zone A: open-plan office; occupancy sensors feed BMS.
- Zone B: meeting rooms; CO2 and temperature sensors.
- Air Handling Units: AHU-1 (Zone A), AHU-2 (Zone B), AHU-3 (Common areas).

Operational Policies:
- Occupancy-based ventilation: reduce airflow when occupancy < 30%.
- Night setback: raise cooling setpoint by 2°C after 8 PM.
- Weekly maintenance windows: Saturday 8–10 AM.
